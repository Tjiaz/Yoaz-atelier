About Me

Hello, I’m Yusuf Olatunji Azeez, a Designer and Front-End Developer specializing in Web Design and Digital Media.

In 2012, I earned my Bachelor of Science in Mathematics, after which i proceeded for Master in Fin Math i finished in 2015 . Since then, I started doing some freelance work with some local clients here in Nigeria. After my Bsc, I was hired in a web development company; which i hone my skills in terms of concepts, color theory, typography, designs, coding standards, and flexibility.

As for my first job, I did a lot of corporate designs and learned XHTML/CSS. Having experienced from my previous companies, later on I moved on to a logistics company with over two years experienced in a related field. The next step of my career was moving to a web development agency as a Web/Graphic Designer and later on promoted to an Acting Project Manager.

I always into learning new things when it comes about web. It will be my playground to enhance every aspects as a creative designer.

My portfolio is just to show case the few works i have done sofar
